About

I'm a Computer & Electronics Engineering student at LASU and the founder of Unify. I attend a computer startup school alongside my degree.

I think of myself as a product person who can build — not the other way around. The most important skill isn't writing code. It's knowing what to build, for whom, and why it matters.

Most of what I build is for people around me — students who are stuck, citizens who have no safe channel to speak, households that deal with power cuts every day. The problem comes first. The tools follow.
